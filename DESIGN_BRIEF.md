# Design Brief

## Style: Secure Shield

The design should evoke a sense of security and reliability, with clean lines, minimal ornamentation, and a focus on functionality. The interface should be intuitive and easy to navigate, with clear typography and ample white space. The overall atmosphere should be professional and trustworthy, conveying the app's commitment to privacy and encryption.

## Design Tokens

| Aspect | Direction |
|--------|-----------|
| Colors | Dark blues and greys to convey a sense of professionalism and security |
| Typography | Sans-serif fonts with a focus on legibility and clarity |
| Components | Simple, rounded buttons and cards with a subtle shadow effect |
| Imagery | Abstract illustrations of locks, shields, and other security-related icons |

## Theme Settings

- **Theme Color:** slate
- **Mode:** dark
- **Border Radius:** 0.3

## App Variant: Echo

> Secure Conversations Matter

Echo is a premium, privacy-focused encrypted messaging app designed for high-stakes professionals and individuals who require absolute security. It offers end-to-end encryption, secure messaging, voice calls, video calls, and groups, all backed by a robust server infrastructure. Echo's unique selling point is its advanced security audit feature, which allows users to monitor and control their data's security.

**Target Audience:** High-stakes professionals and privacy-conscious individuals
**Monetization:** Donations and optional premium support services
**Complexity:** premium
